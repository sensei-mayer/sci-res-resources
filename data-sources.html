Accessing reliable data is key to robust research. Here are some excellent sources for publicly available datasets across various disciplines.

  • General Government & International Databases:
    • Data.gov (US Government Data)
    • UN Data (United Nations Statistics)
    • World Bank Open Data
    • CDC WONDER (Public Health Data)
    • NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI)
    • NASA Open Data Portal
    • US Census Bureau Data
    • Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) Data
    • WHO Global Health Observatory Data
    • Eurostat (European Statistics)
  • Academic & Research Repositories:
    • Kaggle Datasets (Community-contributed data for ML/DS)
    • UCI Machine Learning Repository
    • Figshare (Multidisciplinary Research Data)
    • Zenodo (Open Science Repository)
    • Open Science Framework (OSF)
    • Dryad Digital Repository (Scientific and medical data)
  • Citizen Science & Environmental Data:
    • iNaturalist (Biodiversity Observations)
    • EPA Environmental Data Topics
    • GBIF (Global Biodiversity Information Facility)
    • Cornell Lab of Ornithology Citizen Science
    • Zooniverse (People-powered research)
  • Specific Data Types & Domains:
    • Our World in Data (Global Development & Health)
    • NASA Climate Change Data
    • GenBank (Genetic Sequence Data)
    • The Human Protein Atlas
    • NASA Exoplanet Archive
    • U.S. Energy Information Administration (EIA) Data
  • New York State (NYS) Specific Data:
    • NYS Open Data (Comprehensive NYS data portal)
    • NYS Department of Environmental Conservation (DEC) Data
    • NYS Education Department (NYSED) Data & Research
    • NYS Department of Health (DOH) Community Health Assessment Clearinghouse
    • NYS Department of Labor Statistics
    • Empire State Development (ESD) Research & Data (Economic)
    • NYS Office of the State Comptroller (OSC) Local Government Data
    • NYS Department of State (Licensing & Business Data)
    • New York Power Authority (NYPA) Open Data
