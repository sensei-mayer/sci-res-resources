Effective data collection is fundamental to any research project. This section provides resources on tools for collecting data and best practices for various measurement techniques.

  • Sensors & Hardware for Data Acquisition:

    • SparkFun Electronics (Sensors and components)
    • Adafruit (Sensors, electronics, and tutorials)
    • Vernier Software & Technology (Educational sensors)
  • Software for Sensor Data Logging & Analysis:
    • Arduino IDE (Programming Arduino)
    • Python (with libraries like `pyserial` for Arduino communication)
    • Logger Pro (Software for Vernier sensors)
  • Survey Design & Implementation:
    • Google Forms (Free, easy-to-use survey tool)
    • Qualtrics (Advanced survey platform, often academic licenses)
    • SurveyMonkey: Survey Design Guidelines
    • QuestionPro: Types of Survey Questions
    • APA Ethical Principles of Psychologists and Code of Conduct (relevant for human subjects research)
  • Observational Techniques (Natural & Physical Sciences):
    • Science Buddies: Observational Research Projects
    • National Geographic: Field Observation
    • Nature: Best practices for observational studies
    • Penn State: Remote Sensing and Image Interpretation (for earth sciences)
